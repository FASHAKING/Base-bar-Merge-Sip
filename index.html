🍹 Merge Sip

Slide matching drinks together and mix your way up to the Legendary Tiki!

❓  How to play
  1. Aim & pour: touch and drag left or right to aim, then let go — your drink slides up the sandy board.
  2. Merge: when two identical drinks touch, they mix into a fancier drink and score points. Keep merging toward the Legendary Tiki!
  3. Combos & orders: quick back-to-back merges multiply points (up to ×5). Deliver the drink on the TO-GO ORDER card for a bonus.
  4. Don't overfill: if any drink stops below the dashed line, the bar is backed up — game over.
  5. Onchain: when a round ends, your wallet pops up to serve the score onchain — confirm it and you're on the leaderboard.
